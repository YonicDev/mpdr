Metroid Prime Door Randomizer

Metroid Prime Door Randomizer (also known as MPDR) is a tool to change the types (colors) of doors in Metroid Prime, essentially requiring other Beams to open them.

It also supports adding other patches to the game to help with the flow of the game, mainly for speedrunners and random runners.
